Rustic Christmas Ornaments


What You'll Need:

  • 5-6 Inch Wooden Sticks
  • 3-4 Kinds of Ribbon
  • Twine or String

Begin by cutting ribbon into about 4 inch pieces. Take your longest piece of string, and tie it an inch from the bottom of your stick so that a section hangs on either side. Repeat this step with a slightly smaller piece each time, moving up the stick.

Make sure each ribbon is close together so the "tree" looks full. Once you have the rest of the stick filled with ribbon, trim each ribbon at a diagonal, with the shortest ones at the top and the longests pieces at the bottom. Now you can add the twine or string at the top.

About

I used a festive sparkly gold string for my ornament. Wrap the string around the stick a few times before you tie it. Now you can add a star to the top or other embellishments if you'd like. If you want everything to be very secure, add glue before you tie the ribbons on. Aren't they cute?
