Handmade Christmas Cards


What You'll Need:

  • Cards or cardstock
  • Patterned Paper
  • Mod Podge
  • Metallic Pens
  • Craft Paint

Start with a premade card, or some cardstock folded in half. I like using brown paper for a rustic look. For the snowman themed cards, use a bit of white paint on your pointer finger. For each section of the snowman, press your finger down and swirl in a small circular motion, until you have the size snowball you want. Build your snowman this way, and then add some text.

In the empty space left over, add little white paint dots with the handle end of your bruch. You can add a face, hat, and arms with pen. For the patterned paper cards, cut 2 or 3 different size squares of patterned and solid paper. Layer these squares on the card with Mod Podge. On the final layer add text or paint a cute scene. Viola, the cutest handmade cards!
