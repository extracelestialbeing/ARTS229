Handmade Christmas Cards


What You'll Need:

  • Cards or cardstock
  • Metallic Pens
  • Craft Paint

Start with a premade card, or some cardstock folded in half. I like using brown paper for a rustic look. For the snowman themed cards, use a bit of white paint on your pointer finger. For each section of the snowman, press your finger down and swirl in a small circular motion, until you have the size snowball you want. Build your snowman this way, and then add some text.

In the empty space left over, add little white paint dots with the handle end of your brush. You can add a face, hat, and arms with pen. For the tree themed cards, use a small brush to create a few pine tree shapes. Add a few dabs of white paint to create snow on the trees, and then follow the same steps for the snowflakes and text. Viola, the cutest handmade cards!

Bonus step! If you want to embellish the envelope for some extra festivity, use some white paint and add little white dots as a cute border. I added it all along the edges of the card, and the seams on the back. You can also add a cute little illustration on the front if you want!
