Festive Candy Sleighs


What You'll Need (for one Sleigh):

  • Hot Glue Gun
  • 2 Candy Canes
  • 6 Hershey's Minis
  • 1 Kit Kat Bar
  • Gift Bow

Plug in your hot glue gun and let it heat up. Once heated, start with the Kit Kat bar and two candy canes. Glue both candy canes on either side of the bottom of the Kit Kat. These are the runners on the sleigh. Turn it over, and add a stripe of hot glue. Place three of the Hershey's minis along the glue in the middle of the Kit Kat.

On top of the three Hershey's minis, add another stripe of glue. The next layer has two pieces of candy, and the top layer has just one. I like to alternate what kind of chocolate so the colors alternate as well. Once you have a little Hershey's triangle on top of the Kit Kat, all that's left is adding the bow. That's the finishing touch, now you have a Candy Sleigh!
