Yuletide Orange Garland


What You'll Need:

  • Twine
  • Oranges

First, preheat your oven to about 150 to 170 degrees. Cut your oranges into thin slices, and line an ungreased baking pan with the slices. Drying the orange slices in your oven will take anywhere from 4-8 hours, depending on your oven. Check on them every couple hours so they don't burn.

Once your orange slices are dried, you can start creating the garland. poke a little hole in the center of each slice. Cut your twine a little longer than you want the garland to be. Start by threading the twine through the first slice, moving it down the twine about a foot. Wrap the twine around one half of the slice, and then tie. Repeat this step with all of the slices.
